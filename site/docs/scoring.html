Scoring Methodology

0-100 Score

Each audit produces a score from 0 to 100, where 100 represents perfect onboarding experience and 0 indicates critical issues that prevent user success.

Weights v1

Our scoring algorithm uses weighted categories to calculate the final score:

  • Signup friction: 25%
  • Activation path: 30%
  • Copy & clarity: 20%
  • Momentum: 15%
  • Technical: 10%

Formula (simplified): Score = Σ(weight_i × category_i_subscore). Each category subscore averages its checks, with heavier weight on critical blockers.

Transparency: We publish weights and update them in the changelog. Reports include the build ID and scoring version.
