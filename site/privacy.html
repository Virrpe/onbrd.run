Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 23, 2025

1. What We Collect

We collect minimal data necessary to provide our service:

  • Website URLs you analyze (hashed for privacy)
  • Performance metrics from public web pages
  • Usage analytics (anonymous, aggregated)
  • Account information if you create an account

2. How We Use Data

Your data is used solely to:

  • Generate onboarding audit reports
  • Calculate performance benchmarks
  • Improve our service quality
  • Process payments (via Stripe)

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We only share data with:

  • Stripe for payment processing
  • Legal authorities if required by law

4. Data Retention

We retain audit data for 90 days to maintain benchmark accuracy. Account data is retained until you request deletion.

5. Your Rights

You can:

  • Request access to your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of analytics

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption and access controls.

7. Changes

We may update this policy. Significant changes will be notified via email or in-app notice.
